How To Grow An Orange

Step 1

Soak your orange seed in water for 2 days.

Step 2

Bury your orange seed in a pot of soil.

Step 3

Wet your soil.

Step 4

Wait a few months for your plant to grow

Step 5

Pick your oranges and enjoy!

Tips & Tricks

  • Shine a flashlight on the plant at all times or put it in an area with sun.
  • Make sure to water your plant regularly.
